Protocol 2 (DKA) - Infusion Rate Adjustment

  • Blood glucose > 250 mg/dL: Insulin rate 10 mL/hr, Dextrose none
  • Blood glucose 200-250 mg/dL: Insulin rate 7 mL/hr, Dextrose 2.5%
  • Blood glucose 150-200 mg/dL: Insulin rate 5 mL/hr, Dextrose 2.5%
  • Blood glucose 100-150 mg/dL: Insulin rate 5 mL/hr, Dextrose 5%
  • Blood glucose < 100 mg/dL: Stop insulin, Dextrose 5%

Pressor Monitoring (Norepinephrine Sliding Scale)

Oscillometric Monitoring

  • If MAP > 95 mmHg: decrease norepinephrine by 0.1 mcg/kg/min
  • If MAP 70-95 mmHg: maintain current dose
  • If MAP < 70 mmHg: increase norepinephrine by 0.1 mcg/kg/min

Doppler Monitoring

  • If Doppler BP > 110 mmHg: decrease norepinephrine by 0.1 mcg/kg/min
  • If Doppler BP 80-100 mmHg: maintain current dose
  • If Doppler BP < 80 mmHg: increase norepinephrine by 0.1 mcg/kg/min

Amlodipine Loading

Cat

  • Start with 1/4 tablet (2.5/4 = 0.625 = 0.1 mg/kg), check in 1-2 hours
  • If still high give another 1/4 tablet (0.625 mg), repeat until BP < 180
  • Then in 12 hours repeat total dose that was titrate

Dog

  • 0.1 mg/kg amlodipine PO q 1 hr until BP < 160 mm Hg, or a total of 5 doses are given (0.5 mg/kg)

Goal

  • Minimal goal: prehypertensive SBP < 160
  • End-organ damage noted in dogs SBP > 160 mmHg
  • Ideal goal: normotensive SBP < 140

Maintenance Instructions

  • If systolic BP > 160, give
  • If systolic BP 130-159, ask clinician. report last SBP
  • If systolic BP < 130, skip and alert clinician

Heparin CRI - Titration Protocol

  • 80 U/kg bolus over 2 min, then 600 U/kg/day (25 U/kg/hr)
  • Please record total volume infused in treatment notes (mandatory)
  • < 1.3-1.5 x baseline: add in 400 U/kg/day (41 U/kg/hr)
  • If between 1.3-1.5 x baseline: OK (no change)
  • > 1.5 x baseline: minus 300 U/kg/day (-12.5 U/kg/hr)
  • > 2 x baseline: either stop for 1 hr or -500 U/kg/day (20 U/kg/hr)
  • If close to baseline, give a 20-30 U/kg bolus to bump without increasing CRI rate
